Where we go
Routes connecting Zambia
Operating key freight corridors across Southern Africa — from the Copperbelt to the Atlantic and Indian Ocean ports.
Walvis Bay, Namibia

Atlantic coast port — a critical export hub for landlocked Southern Africa. Long-haul westbound corridor.

Indian Ocean port — a vital corridor for imports and exports through eastern Mozambique via Zimbabwe.

Lubumbashi, DRC

Northbound corridor into the DRC — serving the mineral-rich Katanga region from the Zambian Copperbelt.

Who we are
About Sandhu Transport

Sandhu Transport is a freight and logistics company that plans, implements and operates supply chain solutions on a national, regional and global scale. We are the driving force that keeps goods moving, businesses running, and commitments delivered on time — every time. With a fleet of 90+ trucks and a team of over 130 people, we guarantee seamless operations across borders.

Our Mission

To provide safe, efficient, and cost-effective transport solutions through modern fleet management, skilled personnel, and a customer-first approach — ensuring every delivery is on time, every time.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred transport and logistics partner in the region, recognised for our reliability, safety, and commitment to delivering excellence in every journey.
