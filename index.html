Who we are
About Sandhu Transport

Sandhu Transport is a Freight and Logistics service company that plans, implements and operates supply chain solutions on a national, regional and global scale. We are the driving force that keeps goods moving, businesses running, and commitments delivered on time — every time.

We proudly operate a modern fleet of 90+ trucks and 10 specialized tankers, with a team of over 130 employees united by one goal — delivering value to our clients. Through our comprehensive network of trusted agents, associates, and offices, we guarantee seamless operations across borders.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred transport and logistics partner in the region, recognised for our reliability, safety, and commitment to delivering excellence in every journey.

Where we go
Our routes connecting Zambia
Operating key freight corridors across Southern Africa — from the Copperbelt to the Atlantic and Indian Ocean ports.
Walvis Bay, Namibia

Atlantic coast port — a critical export hub for landlocked Southern African countries. Long-haul westbound corridor.

Windhoek, Namibia

Capital city freight and distribution across Namibia. Connecting Zambia through Botswana and into the Namibian heartland.

Beira, Mozambique

Indian Ocean port — a vital corridor for imports and exports through eastern Mozambique. Eastbound route via Zimbabwe.

Lubumbashi, DRC

Northbound corridor into the Democratic Republic of Congo — serving the mineral-rich Katanga region from the Zambian Copperbelt.
