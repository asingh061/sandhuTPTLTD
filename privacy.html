Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

In short: this is an informational website about our services. We do not operate user accounts, we do not run a contact form, and we do not use advertising or analytics cookies. We have designed the site to collect as little information as possible.

This Privacy Policy explains how Sandhu Transport Limited (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information in connection with our website at sandhutransportzambia.com (the “Website”).

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not ask for, collect, or store personal information through this Website. Specifically:

  • There is no sign-up, login, or user account.
  • There is no contact or enquiry form that submits your details to us.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or remarketing cookies.
  • We do not build profiles of visitors or sell any data.

How You Can Contact Us

When you choose to contact us using the phone, email, or WhatsApp links on the Website, you do so through your own device and applications. Any information you share in that message is provided voluntarily and directly to us so that we can respond to your enquiry. We use it only for that purpose and do not add it to any marketing list.

AI Assistant (“Ask Sandhu AI”)

Our Website includes an AI assistant that answers questions about our services. When you type a question, it is sent to our service provider (OpenAI) to generate a response, which is then shown back to you.

We do not store the questions you type or the answers you receive. Please do not enter personal, sensitive, or confidential information into the assistant. Questions sent to OpenAI are handled under OpenAI’s own policies.

Embedded Map

Our contact section includes a map provided by Google Maps. When the map loads, Google may receive technical information such as your IP address, under Google’s own privacy policy. This is controlled by Google, not by us.

Links to Other Websites

Our Website may link to third-party websites or services that we do not operate. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their policies.

Children’s Privacy

Our Website is intended for a general business audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
